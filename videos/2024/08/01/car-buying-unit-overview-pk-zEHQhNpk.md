Sal Khan welcomes educators to the Car Buying unit, which provides essential information about purchasing or leasing a vehicle. He emphasizes that most people will acquire a car at some point in their life, making this a crucial skill to teach. The unit guides students in considering whether they need a car, what they can afford, and the overall costs of ownership, such as maintenance, fuel, and depreciation. Comparisons of buying versus leasing are also included. Khan then delves into the process of negotiating a price and the various payment methods like cash, financing, and leasing. He encourages teachers to share their personal experiences with their students to enrich the learning experience.
