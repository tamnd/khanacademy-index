A Schumer box is a standardized format for displaying key terms and conditions of a credit card in a way that is easy for consumers to understand. In a Schumer box, information such as the annual percentage rate (APR), annual fees, balance transfer fees, and late payment fees are all laid out in a table format so that consumers can quickly compare different credit card offers.
