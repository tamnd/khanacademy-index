## Description

The State Department of Education in Arizona has announced they are making Khan Academy's AI tools for teachers and students FREE for select districts in the state. Ask for more info at districts.khanacademy.org or email ChelseaHatchard@khanacademy.org

Watch this webinar to
1. get an overview of the forever-free resources available to support prek-12 learning on Khan Academy
2. see how the AI tools can be used to support learning and teaching
3. meet the Khan Academy folks supporting districts in Arizona
