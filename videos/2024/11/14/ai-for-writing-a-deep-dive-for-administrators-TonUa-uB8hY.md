## Description

EdTech Literacy expert Sarah Robertson (Former CommonLit, now Product Leader at Khan Academy) gives a small group of District Administrators a deep dive into how technologists in Silicon Valley are working shoulder to shoulder with educators to solve age-old problems in the ELA classroom with AI. The focus of this webinar is the evolution of Khan Academy's Writing Coach tool for students and the newly released Teacher view. Sarah also shares some free Khanmigo AI tools that are useful for ELA teachers.

Learn more at districts.khanacademy.org
