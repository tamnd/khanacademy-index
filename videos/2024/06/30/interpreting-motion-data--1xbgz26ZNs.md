The same motion data can be presented in various ways, including dot (oil drop) diagrams, position-time tables, and position vs. time graphs. The slope of a position vs. time graph represents the object's velocity: A straight line (constant slope) means the object moved with constant velocity, and a curved line (changing slope) means the object accelerated.
