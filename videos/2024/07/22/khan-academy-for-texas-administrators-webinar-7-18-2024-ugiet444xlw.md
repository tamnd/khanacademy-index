## Description

We don’t have to tell y’all: education isn’t one-size-fits-all. And that’s why we are excited to announce that our FREE, TEKS-aligned courses are officially live and ready for the new school year! Thanks to the generosity of the ExxonMobil Foundation, we have updated our platform to assist Texas students and educators with delivering high-quality, mastery-based learning experiences. 

In this webinar, Dr. Mark Jamison is joined by Dr. Kate Garfinkel to walk district administrators in Texas through the new courses, and how they come together with Khan Academy's teacher and student AI tools to drive big gains for districts. Learn more at districts.khanacademy.org
