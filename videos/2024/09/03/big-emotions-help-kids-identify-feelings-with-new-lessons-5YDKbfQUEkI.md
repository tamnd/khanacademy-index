## Description

Life’s BIG moments come with BIG feelings! The @KhanAcademyKids team is here to help with a new set of lessons all about emotions. Open the free Khan Academy Kids app to play games that help kids manage feelings like happiness, sadness, and everything in between. Join Kodi the Bear to make a face, or practice calming scary thoughts by naming feelings. Expressive artwork, cute new emojis, and playful animations help little learners begin the journey of social-emotional development in a fun and engaging way. 

The new lessons are available for PreK-2nd grade and cover emotions like disappointment, embarrassment, excitement, boredom, and more. To see the new lessons, download the FREE Khan Academy Kids app on Apple, Google Play, or Amazon app stores and go to the Logic+ tab.
