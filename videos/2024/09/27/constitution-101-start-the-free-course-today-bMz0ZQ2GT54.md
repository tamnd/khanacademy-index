## Description

Join Khan Academy founder Sal Khan National and the National Constitution Center President and CEO Jeffrey Rosen as they tell you why they're thrilled to introduce the Constitution 101 student course developed in partnership with the Khan Academy and the National Constitution Center. 

This student-mastery course is designed to empower students with a deeper understanding of the Constitution and its impact on their lives. The course will explore the importance of civil dialogue, how to think like a constitutional scholar, and address timely constitutional questions related to student free speech, AI, and elections.
