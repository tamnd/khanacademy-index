Abiotic factors affect where an organism can occur as well as its survival. Some abiotic factors form physical barriers and restrict where organisms can go, such as mountain ranges or natural phenomena including wildfires and floods. Temperature, light availability, and moisture are all important abiotic factors that influence an organism’s survival.
