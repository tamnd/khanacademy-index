## Description

When going to college or learning after high school, there are different costs to think about, like direct costs for things like tuition and books, and indirect costs for things like housing and food. Some costs are fixed, like application fees, while others can change, like course materials or entertainment, and these costs can be different for community college, four-year college, technical school, online programs, or apprenticeships.
