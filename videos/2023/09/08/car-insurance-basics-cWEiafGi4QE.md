Car insurance has different types of coverage, like liability for when you accidentally hurt someone or damage their property, collision to fix your car after an accident, and comprehensive for other damage like theft or weather. There's also uninsured motorist coverage if someone without insurance hits you, and medical payments to help with medical costs after an accident.
