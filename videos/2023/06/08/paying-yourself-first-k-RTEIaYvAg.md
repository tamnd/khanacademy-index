Paying yourself first means that before you spend any of your money on bills, food, or other expenses, you put some of it into savings. This way, you're making sure that you're saving money each time you get paid, rather than hoping there's something left over at the end of the month. This can be a good way to build up your savings over time and get into the habit of spending less than you earn.
