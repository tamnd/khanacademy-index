There are a number of reasons why you should save money. Having savings can help you be prepared for unexpected expenses, work towards long-term goals, and reduce stress by providing a sense of financial security. To save, it's important to budget and identify areas where you can cut expenses. Automating your savings through direct deposit or apps can help make the process easier, and keeping your savings in a separate account can help you resist the temptation to spend it.
