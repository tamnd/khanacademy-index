## Description

In 2008, Khan Academy started with a mission to provide a free, world-class education to anyone, anywhere. Over time, we've had more than 50 efficacy studies demonstrate that 30 minutes of personalized practice per day throughout a school year can lead to accelerated learning. 

Today, we reach millions of learners and are on the cusp of going even further by providing every learner with a personalized tutor, and every educator with a personalized teacher assistant via AI. However, we can’t do it without help. The Learners Fund provides that critical support. This is our journey to help move humanity forward. 

Share the video and join The Learners Fund today: http://khan.co/EducateInnovators

As a nonprofit, we have a big mission: provide a free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere. And we're serious about it!
