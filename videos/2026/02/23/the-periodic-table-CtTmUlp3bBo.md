The periodic table organizes elements by atomic number, revealing patterns in their physical and chemical properties. Elements are categorized as metals, nonmetals, or metalloids based on distinct behaviors. Vertical groups share similar chemical properties based on their number of valence electrons. The octet rule explains how atoms achieve stability by gaining, losing, or sharing electrons.
