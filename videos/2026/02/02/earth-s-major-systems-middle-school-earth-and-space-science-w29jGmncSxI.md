Earth’s atmosphere, geosphere, hydrosphere, and biosphere work together to shape every place on the planet. Volcanic eruptions formed the Hawaiian Islands, while water, air, and living things slowly changed them. This series of interactions among Earth’s major systems explains how landforms develop and how the planet supports life.
