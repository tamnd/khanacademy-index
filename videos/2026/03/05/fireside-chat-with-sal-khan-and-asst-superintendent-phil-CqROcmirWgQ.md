Join Sal Khan, Founder of Khan Academy and Phil Misecko, Asst. Superintendent of Hanover Community School Corporation of Decatur Township, discussing the practical challenges and opportunities of educational technology. Dr. Misecko shares candid insights into the budgetary pressures facing districts and how innovative tools can help maintain high-quality instruction with limited resources. The conversation explores the role of AI in providing immediate, constructive feedback for math and writing, effectively acting as a personal tutor to prevent bad practice habits. By focusing on teacher engagement and academic outcomes, they highlight how purposeful technology adoption can bridge learning gaps and support differentiated instruction at scale.
