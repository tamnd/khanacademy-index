## Description

Khan Academy is reimagining student assessment by moving beyond stagnant data to provide instructional leaders with actionable, real-time insights. Using Khanductor, a new, socratic AI tool, the platform engages students in dialogue to uncover unfinished learning and deeper reasoning that traditional tests often miss. These assessments automatically generate personalized learning paths within the Khan Academy ecosystem, offering every student a clear, individualized roadmap to grade-level mastery. By focusing on qualitative reporting and student agency, this solution equips administrators to accelerate learning recovery and ensure on-time graduation.
