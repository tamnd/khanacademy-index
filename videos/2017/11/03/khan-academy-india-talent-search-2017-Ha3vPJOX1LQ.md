## Description

Learn more and submit your videos: https://www.khanacademy.org/r/indiatalentsearch2017

Khan Academy is looking for brilliant explainers whose videos help students succeed in school and find joy in learning.

We are looking for submissions in Math and Science for Std 6-12 level in English, Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, and Marathi. If you have a passion for teaching and a knack for making challenging concepts seem simple, send us one of your videos!

Each language will have one winner and one runner-up. All 10 winners will be considered for video creation contracts with Khan Academy. In addition, they will receive cash prizes and have their videos published on Khan Academy.

Send us your video today!
