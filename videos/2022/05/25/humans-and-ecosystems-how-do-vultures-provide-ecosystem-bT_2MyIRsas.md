The Lammergeier, a bone-eating vulture, exemplifies the importance of biodiversity and ecosystem services. These services, categorized into provisioning, regulating, supporting, and cultural, sustain the environment and human life. However, human disruptions, like harmful chemicals, can endanger species like vultures, leading to ecological and economic consequences.
