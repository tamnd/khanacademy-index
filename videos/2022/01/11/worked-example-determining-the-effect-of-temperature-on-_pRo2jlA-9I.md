## Description

When ΔH° and ΔS° for a reaction have the same sign, the thermodynamic favorability of the reaction depends on temperature. In this video, we'll determine the thermodynamic favorability of a reaction with negative values of ΔH° and ΔS° at two different temperatures. We'll also calculate the temperature at which the reaction changes from being thermodynamically favored to thermodynamically unfavored.
