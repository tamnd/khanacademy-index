## Description

Standard cell potential is the potential of a cell when all reactants and products are in their standard states. The standard potential for any cell can be calculated by subtracting the standard reduction potential of the half-reaction occurring at the anode from the standard reduction potential of the half-reaction occurring at the cathode.
