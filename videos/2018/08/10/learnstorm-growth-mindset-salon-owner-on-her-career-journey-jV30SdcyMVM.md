## Description

Listen to Sam DiVine, a Salon Owner, talk about her journey to discovering that the beauty industry could be a great career path and how she found her own path within the industry. Sam's story is part of the Growth Mindset Curriculum available with LearnStorm, a back-to-school program aimed at helping students start the school year strong. The growth mindset curriculum helps students take their own life experiences apply their learnings in the face of frustration, making mistakes and learning new things.
