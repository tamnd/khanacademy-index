## Description

Listen to Paul Clifton, a teacher leader, talk about why he wanted to become a teacher and how he is passionate about becoming a role model as a black male in the middle school classroom. Paul's story is part of the Growth Mindset Curriculum available with LearnStorm, a back-to-school program aimed at helping students start the school year strong. The growth mindset curriculum helps students take their own life experiences apply their learnings in the face of frustration, making mistakes and learning new things.
