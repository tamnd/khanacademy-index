## Description

Listen to Melanie Fox, Khan Academy's economics content creator talk about how she got through one of her hardest classes in college. Melanie's story is part of the Growth Mindset Curriculum available with LearnStorm, a back-to-school program aimed at helping students start the school year strong. The growth mindset curriculum helps students take their own life experiences apply their learnings in the face of frustration, making mistakes and learning new things.
