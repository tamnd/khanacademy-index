## Description

Listen to Kim Kutz Elliott, Khan Academy's humanities content creator talk about her struggles in college to find social belonging. She talks about how she eventually got over the "imposter syndrome" and became successful socially. Kim's story is part of the Growth Mindset Curriculum available with LearnStorm, a back-to-school program aimed at helping students start the school year strong. The growth mindset curriculum helps students take their own life experiences apply their learnings in the face of frustration, making mistakes and learning new things.
