## Description

Listen to Dave Paunesku, the executive director of PERTS, talk about addressing student effort in an effective way. PERTS is a center at Stanford University that expands on the research done by Carol Dweck on growth mindset. The Growth Mindset Curriculum, created in partnership with PERTS, is available with LearnStorm, a back-to-school program aimed at helping students start the school year strong. The growth mindset curriculum helps students take their own life experiences apply their learnings in the face of frustration, making mistakes and learning new things.
