## Description

Do you wish that your students could overcome frustration or deal with mistakes in a healthy, positive way? Have you wanted to implement growth mindset in your classroom but need a few pointers? Find out how you can foster growth mindset in your classroom this school year with LearnStorm growth mindset activities. 

Three experts in the growth mindset research field will explain what it’s all about: 
- Dave Paunesku: Founding director of PERTS, a center at Stanford University that helps educators apply insights from the behavioral sciences to create safe and engaging learning environments to foster student motivation and resilience. We partnered with Dave and PERTS for the development of the LearnStorm growth mindset activities.
- Annie Brock: A former teacher and author of The Growth Mindset Coach, a month-by-month guide to implementing growth mindset in your classroom. 
- Samala Robinson: A current fourth-grade teacher in Independence, Missouri who has developed creative ways to foster positive learning mindsets.  

Khan Academy hosted this free growth mindset livestream on September 20 at 6:00 p.m. EST/3:00 p.m. PDT.
