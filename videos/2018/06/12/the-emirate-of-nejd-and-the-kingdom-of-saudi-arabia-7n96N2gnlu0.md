## Description

After the fall of the Emirate of Diriyah in 1818, in 1824 the second Saudi state known as the Emirate of Nejd is established. It last until 1891 when it fall to the Rashidun tribe. The third Saudi state is then established in 1902 by Abdulaziz which becomes the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, which became a major player on the global stage with the discovery of major oil reserves in 1938.
