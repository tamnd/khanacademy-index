A deep dive into Article V of the US Constitution, which establishes the amendment process. In this video, Kim Kutz Elliott discusses Article V with scholars Michael Rappaport and David Strauss.

To read more, visit the National Constitution Center’s Interactive Constitution. On this site, leading scholars interact and explore the Constitution and its history.
