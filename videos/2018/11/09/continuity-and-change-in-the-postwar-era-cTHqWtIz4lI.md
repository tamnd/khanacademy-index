How much did the events of the tumultuous postwar era reshape American national identity? Kim Kutz Elliott discusses the extent to which developments like the Cold War, the Civil Rights Movement, Vietnam, and student protests of the 1960s changed core beliefs around citizenship, cultural values, and the proper US role in the world.
