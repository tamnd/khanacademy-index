The equilibrium constant for a reaction depends on how the balanced equation is written. If the equation is reversed, K is inverted. If the equation is multiplied by a factor n, K is raised to the nth power. If multiple equations are added together, K for the overall equation is the product of the K values for the equations that were summed.
