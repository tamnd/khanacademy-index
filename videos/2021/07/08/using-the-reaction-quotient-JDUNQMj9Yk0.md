## Description

By comparing the reaction quotient to the equilibrium constant, we can predict the direction a reaction will proceed to reach equilibrium. If Q is less than K, the reaction will proceed towards the products. If Q is greater than K, the reaction will proceed towards the reactants. If Q is equal to K, the reaction is already at equilibrium and will not change.
