## Description

The percent ionization of a weak acid, HA, is defined as the ratio of the equilibrium H₃O⁺ concentration to the initial HA concentration, multiplied by 100%. In this video, we'll use this relationship to find the percent ionization of acetic acid in a 0.20 M solution.
