## Description

Weak bases partially ionize in water to produce hydroxide ions. Because the ionization is not complete, the concentration of OH⁻ in a weak base solution is typically much less than the initial base concentration. The relative strength of a weak base is described by the base ionization constant, Kb, which is the equilibrium constant for the reaction of the weak base with water.
