## Description

A physical or chemical process can be represented using an energy diagram, which shows how the potential energy of the initial state relates to the potential energy of the final state. If the initial state has a lower potential energy than the final state, the process is endothermic. If the initial state has a higher potential energy than the final state, the process is exothermic.
