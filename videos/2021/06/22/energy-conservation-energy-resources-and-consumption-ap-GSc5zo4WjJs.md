## Description

Some of the methods for conserving energy around the home include adjusting the thermostat to reduce the use of heat and air conditioning, conserving water, use of energy efficient appliances, and conservation landscaping.  Methods for conserving energy on a large scale include improving fuel economy for vehicles, using BEV's and hybrid vehicles, using public transportation, and implementing green building design features.
