Indoor air pollutants can come from natural sources, human-made sources, and combustion. Common natural pollutants include radon, mold, and dust. Common human-made pollutants include Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) and formaldehyde. Common combustion pollutants include carbon monoxide, nitrogen oxides, sulfur dioxide, particulates, and tobacco smoke.
