## Description

The results of an acid–base titration can be summarized using a titration curve, which plots pH vs. volume of titrant added. For the titration of a strong acid with a strong base, the curve begins acidic and then turns basic after the equivalence point, which occurs at pH = 7. For the titration of a strong base with a strong acid, the curve is similar except that it begins basic and turns acidic.
