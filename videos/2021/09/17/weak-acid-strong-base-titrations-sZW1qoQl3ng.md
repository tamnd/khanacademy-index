## Description

For the titration of a weak acid with a strong base, the pH curve is initially acidic and has a basic equivalence point (pH is greater than 7). The section of curve between the initial point and the equivalence point is known as the buffer region. At the half-equivalence point, the concentrations of the buffer components are equal, resulting in pH = pKₐ.
