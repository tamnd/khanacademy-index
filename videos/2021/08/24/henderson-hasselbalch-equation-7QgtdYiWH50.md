One way to determine the pH of a buffer is by using the Henderson–Hasselbalch equation, which is pH = pKₐ + log([A⁻]/[HA]). In this equation, [HA] and [A⁻] refer to the equilibrium concentrations of the conjugate acid–base pair used to create the buffer solution. When [HA] = [A⁻], the solution pH is equal to the pKₐ of the acid.
