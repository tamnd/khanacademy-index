## Description

LearnStorm is just that: a collaborative, tech-enabled storm of learning that the whole class can get involved in regardless of their level! As your whole class completes assignments together, they'll watch as the LearnStorm tracker fills in — be prepared for surprise celebration moments! It's free, it's happening in the fall, and best of all: it gives your students a fun way to gain skills with high-quality content, build teamwork, and foster growth mindsets. Find out more on learnstorm.khanacademy.org.
