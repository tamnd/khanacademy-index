## Description

We are excited to share with you good news from Khan Academy! 

We’ve launched Official Praxis® Core Prep - a free, online test prep resource for aspiring teachers to prepare for the Praxis® Core exams! 

Khan Academy has developed this free test prep resource in collaboration with ETS, the makers of the tests.  With its help, aspiring teachers will gain the knowledge and skills they need to succeed on test day and in their future classrooms.  

This resource is now available for free at https://www.khanacademy.org/praxis-core
