An alloy is a mixture of two or more elements, of which at least one is a metal. There are two main types of alloys: interstitial alloys, which form between atoms of different radii, and substitutional alloys, which form between atoms of similar radii. In this video, we'll learn how to represent the different types of alloys using particulate models.
