## Description

Jason spent 15 years in prison. While there, he started learning math from transcripts of Khan Academy videos that his mom printed for him. He now studies computer science at Stanford. Stories like Jason’s are why Khan Academy exists. It shows how a free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere can truly unlock potential. Today, Jason is asking you to help him fundraise for Khan Academy as a way to say thank you. Will you join Jason in going #AllinforEducation?
