## Description

Two of the most common questions we've gotten this year:

1) How does Sal make those videos?

2) How can I do the same???

To help you learn all about the world of tablets and virtual whiteboards, we've brought in the experts — Wacom (which makes the tablet Sal uses) and Stacey Roshan (a math teacher and author of "Tech with Heart").

During this highly actionable session, you'll learn exactly how Sal makes his videos, how you can do the same, and even how you can use a tablet and Khan Academy to walk your students through any exercise.
