## Description

Parents, are you looking for ways to keep your young children (ages 2-7) engaged in learning while at home? Join us for a fireside chat with Caroline Hu Flexer, CEO and Co-Founder of Khan Academy Kids, and Renee Scott, an Early Education Program director at Stanford University. Caroline and Renee cover some easy ways to boost learning throughout the day. They also discuss how to incorporate key language, literacy, numeracy, and social emotional skills into everyday activities and play. And, you’ll likely be surprised that you’re already doing some of these brain boosting activities.
