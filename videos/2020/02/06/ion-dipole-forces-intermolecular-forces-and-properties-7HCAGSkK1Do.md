Ion–dipole forces occur between ions and polar molecules. The relative strength of these forces can be understood in terms of Coulomb's law, which tells us that the electrostatic attraction between ion and dipole is directly related to the magnitudes of the ion charge and the dipole and inversely related to the distance between them.
