## Description

It is the not-too-distant dystopian future. You live in the cyberpunk underbelly of Metropoluxe with your canine companion-bot GoodBoy3000. 

Focus virtue: GRIT in the face of repeated failure

Written by Diana Rosile and Alyssa Foote
Performed by Ken Jones
Sound designed by David Rheinstrom
Illustrated by Alyssa Foote

Khaffeine is a Social and Emotional Learning-focused audio journey that revitalizes and excites learners who are looking for a sense of inspiration despite their daily challenges.

Khaffeine perks you up and gets you going! It encourages the listener to believe in themselves, even—especially!—when circumstances are hardest.
