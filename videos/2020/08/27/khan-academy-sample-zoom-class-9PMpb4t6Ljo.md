## Description

Want to see how to integrate Khan Academy with your live video sessions?

I'm going to teach a sample lesson to show you how to:

-Reduce lecture time during live sessions
-Make your sessions highly interactive
-Drill down on the areas where your students need the most help

PS: Our advice is video conference platform agnostic. So whether you use Zoom, Google Meet, WebEx, or anything else, all the advice will still apply!
