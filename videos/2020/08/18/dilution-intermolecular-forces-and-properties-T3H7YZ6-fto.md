## Description

A common method of making a solution of a given concentration involves taking a more concentration solution and adding water until the desired concentration is reached. This process is known as dilution. We can relate the concentrations and volumes before and after a dilution using the following equation: M₁V₁ = M₂V₂ where M₁ and V₁ represent the molarity and volume of the initial concentrated solution and M₂ and V₂ represent the molarity and volume of the final diluted solution.
