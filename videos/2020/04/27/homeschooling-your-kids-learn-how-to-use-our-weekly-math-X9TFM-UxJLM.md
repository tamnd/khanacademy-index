## Description

Parents, for many of you, your school may be closed through the end of the year, and you may be wondering how to keep your child learning while at home. We're here to help. We've created step-by-step sample weekly math schedules for grades 3 - Algebra 2, and designed them to be a fun, structured way to keep kids learning and reduce the impact of the summer slide and school closures. In this webinar recording, we'll walk you through the schedules and give tips on how you can adapt them for your family.
