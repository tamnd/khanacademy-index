## Description

Tim Vandenberg has been teaching 6th grade in Hesperia, California, for nearly 20 years. But it's only been in the last three years that he's been able to help his students (100% Free/Reduced Lunch, 90% Below Grade Level in Math) outperform not just their starting points - but students across the entire state.

Come learn how Tim was able to bend the arc of his students' trajectories through the power of differentiation, mastery learning, and yes... starting over with kindergarten!
