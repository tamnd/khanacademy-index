## Description

We know this is a tough time for teachers and students alike — especially when it comes to sparking the joy of learning.

That's why we're excited to show you "Pixar in a Box" — a collaboration between Khan Academy and Pixar Animation Studios that's all about generating curiosity and excitement, even in tough times.

Covering a range of subjects — from math to science to the humanities — your students will learn how to animate bouncing balls, build a swarm of robots, and make virtual fireworks explode!

And you'll learn directly from an expert Khan Academy teacher who uses this curriculum with her own students.
