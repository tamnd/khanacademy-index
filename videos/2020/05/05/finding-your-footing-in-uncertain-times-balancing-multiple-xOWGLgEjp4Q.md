## Description

We've heard lots of questions from parents about how to juggle the needs of multiple kids, while being productive at work, and managing a hundred other tasks... all while staying (relatively) sane during an apocalypse. Join us as we chat with Dr. Lindsay Portnoy, an education professor at Northeastern, a parent of multiple kids, and an author with the Association for Supervision and Curriculum Development. She shares her top tips around building clarity, curation, creativity, compassion, and curiosity for parents to support their kids and themselves.
