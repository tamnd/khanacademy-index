## Description

Join Khan Academy Principal Product Manager for Literacy & Classroom Experience, Sarah Robertson, as she takes a deep dive into the new and improved Khan Academy Writing Coach. 

Sarah presents the challenges in teaching writing, the role of AI in learning versus cheating, and how Writing Coach can support students through the writing process. The discussion covers the features of Writing Coach, its instructional purpose, teacher reporting, and ethical considerations of AI in classrooms.
