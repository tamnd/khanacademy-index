## Description

This informative video describes how to effectively support your children as they navigate challenges while using Khan Academy. Learning can be tough, but with the right strategies, you can help your child overcome obstacles and thrive in their studies. In this video, we discuss the importance of patience, practical steps to take when your child feels stuck, and how to encourage them to revisit instructional videos, utilize hints, and break down problems into manageable parts. We also highlight the significance of celebrating their progress to boost confidence and motivation. Your support can make a significant difference in your child's learning journey. Watch now to discover how you can help them develop resilience and succeed in their studies! Happy learning!
