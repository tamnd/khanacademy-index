## Description

A bakery, a lightning bolt, and a very burned cookie tray. Insurance can’t stop disasters—but it can help you bounce back. From deductibles and premiums to moose-related car damage (yes, really), we’re breaking down how insurance works, what kinds you need, and why it’s your financial oven mitt when life gets too hot to handle.
