## Description

In today’s climate of burnout and overwhelm, joy can feel like a luxury. But what if it’s actually your most strategic leadership tool? In this energizing and practical session with Khan Academy Senior Partnerships Manager, Danielle Sullivan, we’ll explore the neuroscience behind joy, gratitude, and play—and why these “soft skills” are anything but soft.
You’ll leave with actionable culture-shifting strategies like Funny First Fridays, Gratitude Tag Boards, Joy Walks, and AI-powered time-savers that help school leaders and educators reclaim energy and connection. Whether you're a principal, coach, or classroom teacher, you’ll walk away with a toolkit for sparking momentum—without toxic positivity or extra prep.
Because joy isn’t fluff—it’s fuel.
