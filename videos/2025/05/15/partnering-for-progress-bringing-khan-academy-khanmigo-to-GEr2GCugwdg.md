## Description

Thank you for watching our recorded webinar from 5/14/25 on partnering with Khan Academy through BOCES. With over 400 district partners across the U.S., Khan Academy’s Districts program is designed to help more students reach grade-level success through mastery learning—now powered by AI tools that support persistence with challenging material.

Email DanielleSullivan@khanacademy.org to bring Khanmigo to your schools. 

In this session, we walk through how the Khan Academy District Partnership goes beyond the free version of Khan Academy with features and services designed specifically for schools and districts:

Khanmigo – Our AI-powered Socratic tutor and teaching assistant that supports students 24/7 and helps teachers save time

Automated rostering – Integration with Clever or ClassLink makes classroom setup seamless

Implementation support – Dedicated guidance from a Khan Academy District Success Manager

Admin reporting dashboards – Track student progress, usage, and engagement with real-time insights

Whether you’re just starting to explore a partnership or looking for ways to scale success, this recording will give you a clear understanding of how Khan Academy can support your district's learning goals.

Learn more at districts.khanacademy.org
