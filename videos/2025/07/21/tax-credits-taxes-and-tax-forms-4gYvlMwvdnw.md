Tax credits directly reduce the amount of tax you owe and can sometimes lead to a refund. Common credits include the Child Tax Credit, Earned Income Tax Credit, and education credits like the American Opportunity Credit. Unlike deductions, which lower taxable income, credits provide a dollar-for-dollar reduction in your tax bill.
