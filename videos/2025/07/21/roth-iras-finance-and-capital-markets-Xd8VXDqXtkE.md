A Roth IRA is a special retirement account where you pay taxes upfront on contributions. It offers flexibility, allowing withdrawals of original contributions without taxes or penalties. At retirement, withdrawals are tax-free. It's different from a traditional IRA, where taxes are deferred until withdrawal.
